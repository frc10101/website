Meet Our Robot

Whoops Y Daisy

Meet our latest competition robot, designed and built for the 2024 FRC season. Whoops Y Daisy highlights our team's engineering prowess with a brand-new funnel stage that keeps chorals flowing to the scorer.

Whoops - Our Swerve Drivetrain

Swerve drive for maximum maneuverability

Y - Our Funnel

Guides chorals into Daisy, staging them for consistent scoring runs

Daisy - Our End Effector

Scores choral and has a beam break to detect when a choral is loaded
